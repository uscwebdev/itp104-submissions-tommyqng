Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is there a requirement to join?

A: Just be Vietnamese.


Q: Do I get extracurricular points count toward my transcript by participating in VSA?

A: Absolutely, this is a very good opportunity to gain those points.


Q: I am Vietnamese American and do not know Vietnamese, would I be able to get along well to everybody?

A: Don't be discouraged just be because you do not know Vietnamese. We are absolutely welcoming. We also have a good portion of Americans in our group.


Q: Will there be auditions for the Lunar New Year performances?

A: Yes, but don't worry. Most of us have no experience, and we have plenty of time to practice. No judging at all.


Q: Is there any kind of fees or dues in order to join?

A: Not at all. We are fully funded by the school.
