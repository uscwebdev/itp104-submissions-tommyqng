Gathering Night

Join us for a variety of multiplayer games while meeting other students. Sharpen your focus while you relax and have fun! Raffle prizes! We host this event at the beginning of the school year for new Vietnamese students to get to know each other and make friends. There will be Vietnamese food and interesting icebreakers. There is no need to sign up or anything. Just come and have fun with us.
