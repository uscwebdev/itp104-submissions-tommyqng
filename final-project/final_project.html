OUR MISSION

The EF Academy's Vietnamese Student Association (aka Vietnamese Gang) strives to serve both the student body and the entire school community. Through the promotion of cultural projects and events, we hope to enrich campus life and become a significant part of the diverse high school community.

We want to celebrate our Vietnamese heritage, values, and traditions, as well as educate and share it with those interested in Vietnamese culture.

We want to reinforce the values we hold dear, as they form the structure of our culture and community.

We want to provide an open and welcoming space for our members, not only for social activities, but also for the discussion of important topics and issues.

We want to connect with and reach out to the broader Vietnamese community outside of campus and maintain a strong network that everyone can participate in.
